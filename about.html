About SeAH Steel UAE LLC

Built on legacy. Driven by innovation.

Who We Are

Founded in 2011 in Ras Al Khaimah, SeAH Steel UAE LLC is a regional arm of SeAH Steel Corp. (est. 1960, South Korea), a leader in steel pipe manufacturing globally. We produce high-quality carbon steel welded pipes tailored to global infrastructure needs.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative and reliable steel solutions that support safe, sustainable, and long-lasting infrastructure development worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the most respected and sustainable steel pipe manufacturer in the Middle East by embracing excellence, integrity, and innovation.
