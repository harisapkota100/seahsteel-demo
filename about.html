About SeAH Steel UAE LLC

Built on legacy. Driven by innovation.

Who We Are

Established in 2011 in Ras Al Khaimah, UAE, SeAH Steel UAE LLC is a regional arm of SeAH Steel Corp. (est. 1960, South Korea). We specialize in manufacturing high-quality carbon steel welded pipes, meeting international standards with precision and efficiency.

Our Mission

To provide superior steel pipe solutions that empower infrastructure across the globe, ensuring unmatched quality, safety, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted steel pipe manufacturer in the region, driving sustainable industrial development and long-term partnerships.
