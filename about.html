About SeAH Steel UAE LLC

Built on legacy. Driven by innovation.

Who We Are

SeAH Steel UAE LLC, established in 2011 in Ras Al Khaimah, is a critical regional manufacturing arm of SeAH Steel Holdings, a global steel leader founded in South Korea in 1960. With a strong presence across Asia, the United States, and Europe, SeAH is globally recognized for producing carbon steel and stainless steel tubes and pipes of the highest quality. SeAH Steel UAE was strategically launched to meet the growing demand for premium-grade steel pipes in the Middle East, North Africa, and beyond. Our facility was built with a significant investment to ensure a high-tech production environment capable of serving energy, construction, water transmission, and infrastructure sectors with absolute precision and reliability. As part of a multinational group, SeAH Steel UAE combines global standards with local responsiveness, enabling us to deliver unmatched steel pipe solutions to some of the region‚Äôs most ambitious projects.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide technically advanced and durable steel pipe solutions that not only meet but exceed customer expectations. We aim to support long-term infrastructure development across oil & gas, water, and industrial sectors through safe, efficient, and environmentally conscious production practices. By continually innovating and improving our manufacturing systems, we ensure reliability, compliance, and excellence in every product we deliver. Customer satisfaction, operational integrity, and sustainable growth are the pillars that guide every step of our operations.

Our Vision

We envision SeAH Steel UAE as a regional leader in steel manufacturing that embodies sustainability, innovation, and excellence. Our vision is to be recognized not only as a manufacturer but also as a long-term partner contributing to the development of critical infrastructure across the Middle East and Africa. We aim to achieve this by integrating smart manufacturing, quality assurance, digital transformation, and environmental stewardship into our daily operations. Through continuous investment in technology and human capital, we strive to create a manufacturing ecosystem that aligns with global best practices and contributes meaningfully to industrial growth and regional development.
